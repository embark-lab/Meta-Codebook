
The **ED100k-Plus** is a lab adapted version of the Ed100k. It is a self
report measure that assesses current and history of an eating disorder
and includes items from the [Dieting and Weight History
Questionnaire](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/23220357/) ( variables
start with wdqhq in measure), ED History, [Eating Disorder
Examination-Questionnaire](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/15033501/), .
It includes questions about binge eating, compensatory behaviors,
restriction and other eating disorder symptoms. This measure is not yet validated. 

**Scoring**

Diagnostic variables are scored per the DSM-5 criteria for threshold and sub-clinical (past and current) Anorexia Nervosa, Atypical Anorexia Nervosa Bulimia Nervosa, Binge Eating Disorder. 

Diagnostic variable are rated on 0,1 scale where 0 = no diagnosis and 1 = diagnosis.

**Key Variables**
`dx_an_current_fem_plus`- Current Anorexia Nervosa diagnosis for females 
`dx_an_history_fem_plus` - History of Anorexia Nervosa diagnosis for females 
`dx_atyp_an_current_plus` - Current Atypical Anorexia Nervosa diagnosis
`dx_atyp_an_history_plus` - History of Atypical Anorexia Nervosa
`dx_bn_history_plus` - History of Bulimia Nervosa 
`dx_bn_current_plus` - Current Bulimia Nervosa diagnosis
`dx_bn_nopurge_hist_plus` - History of Bulimia Nervosa (No Purging)
`dx_bn_nopurge_cur_plus` - Current Bulimia Nervosa (No Purging) 
`dx_bed_history_plus` - History of Binge Eating Disorder diagnosis
`dx_bed_current_plus` - Current Binge Eating Disorder diagnosis
`dx_sub_bed_history_plus` - History of Subthreshold Binge Eating Disorder diagnosis
`dx_sub_bed_current_plus` - Current Subthreshold Binge Eating Disorder diagnosis
`dx_purging_history_plus` - History of Purging Disorder diagnosis
`dx_purging_current_plus` - Current Purging Disorder diagnosis
